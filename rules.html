Rules

These are a few set of guidelines that you should follow while doing games in #cwexperts. These aren't really strict rules just simple and reasonable things to do in response to the current state of the channel over the past few weeks.

1. No Drama

You're doing Castle Wars with friends and others, even if you don't like them just keep the chat light without huge arguments. The requirement is already long enough, do not make it worse for yourself and others. Don't be a cunt and you won't get to see the wrath of Abdel.

2. New Members

I understand you are new but once you've done a few games, you are expected to learn how to lead and salt. You will not leech your way to your requirement, you are bound to get picked eventually. Nobody should pass a role with the excuse that they are "new" unless they are literally fresh off the boat new.

3. Too Busy Can't Lead/Salt

Sometimes you can be really busy like really so busy that you can't do any roles, which is fine. Although, I sometimes don't get some people's definition of busy when I used to do roles while studying for my exams. But if you really can't, then there's no problem at all but if you using this excuse frequently then you will be called out for it and for leeching. There shouldn't be anyone who ALWAYS has an excuse for passing a role and everyone has to do it at some point.

If you are already salt and you get picked to lead, you are supposed to lead and pick a new salt. Do not pass lead just because you "already" have a role. The game cannot keep going without a lead, if you are able and you already salt, you will take the lead and instantly pick a new salt.

4. Messing Up

If you mess up something, like not calling unlobby or hop world in time, it happens. Everyone makes mistakes, but if you deliberatly do mistakes to not do a specific role then you will be called out for it. And if you are having a hard time, don't just pass the role if you don't know how to do, you should learn how to properly because everyone else can.

Leave the roles to the people who are given the roles, for example if someone calls hop world few seconds, there shouldn't be tons of members trying calling hop world at the same time instead of the lead. So unless the lead/salt are completely AFK or disconnected after a whole minute has passed, feel free to call yourself. Since this doesn't apply to lobbying, you have almost a full minute to unlobby so remember the lead still has to get into the game before calling which may take a few seconds. Stop panicing and please let the people who are given roles to do them.

As for members who constantly argue and rage at others for messing up a few seconds to a minute, it's really unnecessary and you should refer to Rules #1 and #2 about this. A few seconds won't kill you since mistakes happen sometimes, for e.g if someone was late or if someone disconnected.
