How to Play

To easily understand how our games work, you need to understand the concept. Basically, we start a game on one world and after 10 minutes, we start a different game on another world with our losers while the original game is still going on. Like that, we can do games in ~13 minutes instead of 23 minutes full games, hence getting around 9-10 tickets per hour.
