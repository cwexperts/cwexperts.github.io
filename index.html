Welcome to CwExperts!

Play 13 minute Castle Wars games at CwExperts for almost 10 gold tickets per hour! Essentially, halving the time needed to complete your Trimmed Completionist cape requirement!
In order to play, you will need a loser (at least level 90 combat, preferably 100-110) and a registered nick on SwiftIRC.

Getting Started

Connect to our channel on SwiftIRC to get started today. You can use any IRC client of your choice such as SwiftKit and mIRC, alternatively you can use the web client we provide below. Simply choose a nickname (preferably your RSN) and press Connect to join our main channel.

IRC Registration

To register your chosen nick on SwiftIRC, follow the steps below:

  1. Once you've connected, you can now register your nick! If your nick is not available or you want to change it, do the following:
    /nick NEW_NICKNAME
  2. If you are happy with your current nick and it's available, you can register it with the following command:
    /ns register PASSWORD PASSWORD EMAIL EMAIL
  3. After executing the command, you should receive an confirmation code to the email address you used to register. Check your email and do the following command:
    /ns confirm CODE
  4. Now you've successfully registered, in order to verify your identity when you connect do the following:
    /ns id PASSWORD
